The first 90 days

Sequenced so nothing depends on something that does not exist yet. Build the spine, add the moat, then widen. Each phase has a definition of done so you know it is actually finished.

Weeks 1 to 4 / Build the spine
  • Film batch one (one 90-minute session, 8 episodes). Start with origin 01 to 04 and recognition 05, 07, 09, 11.
  • Post 2 to 3 per week off that batch. Establish the voice and the cadence.
  • Set the site hero to the locked positioning line; add the short founder origin and a link.
  • Run Maddy's first per-piece consent passes (Section 01) for any named or her-words usage.
Done when: 8+ videos live, posting rhythm holding, site hero and origin updated, consent logged for anything featuring Maddy.
Weeks 5 to 8 / Add the moat
  • Film batch two (problem + build-in-public + the "+": 06, 08, 10, 12, 15, 16, 18, 19).
  • Begin real engagement with 10 to 15 ND-affirming OTs and coaches. No pitch yet.
  • Send the first warm DMs (Section 05 draft). Goal: 3 to 5 genuine conversations.
  • Identify one ND-affirming OT willing to review the one-pager. This clears the gate.
  • Draft the practitioner one-pager so it is ready the moment a reviewer says yes.
Done when: batch two posting, 3 to 5 live practitioner conversations, one-pager drafted, at least one reviewer candidate identified.
Weeks 9 to 13 / Widen and convert
  • Film batch three (caregiver-direct + values + remaining "+": 13, 14, 17, 20, 21, 22, 23, 24).
  • Cut the YouTube origin film from the origin episodes; publish it as the evergreen home.
  • One creator collaboration, micro tier, if a real relationship has formed.
  • Start adding waitlist-link CTAs to every third or fourth video and watch the warm signups arrive.
  • If 500+ engaged followers, run the first build-in-public live.
Done when: full 24-episode bank live, YouTube origin film published, first practitioner reading the one-pager, warm waitlist signups appearing in Supabase, first live done or scheduled.

What to measure

Reach is a vanity trap for this product. The plan converts on trust, so measure trust, depth, and warm intent, not views.

The dashboard that matters

Follows per video, from the right people Saves + shares the "this is me" signal Practitioner convos live, real, moving Warm waitlist signups tagged by source Comment quality recognition, not just volume Maddy comfort the non-negotiable

The weekly review (15 minutes, Sunday)

  • Which video drove the most saves and the best comments. Make more of that.
  • How many practitioner relationships moved a stage. If zero for two weeks, that is the flag to act on.
  • Warm waitlist signups this week, and which story they followed in from.
  • Did anything with Maddy in it cost her comfort. If yes, stop and fix before anything else.

How it fails (so it does not)

Naming the failure modes and their early tells is how you avoid them.
