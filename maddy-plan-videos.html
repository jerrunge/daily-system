A / Origin (the spine story)

01"She could fly across the world alone but could not start the dishes."▸
Aim
caregiver the contradiction that earns the follow
Hook
"My daughter could fly across the world alone but could not start the dishes. Here is what I finally understood."
First 3s
You, direct to camera, no intro. On screen: "She can cross the world alone. She can't start the dishes."
Beats
0-3s the contradiction. 3-15s the two side by side (the big thing she is capable of, the small thing that stops her). 15-35s the insight: novel tasks come with structure, urgency, a destination; daily tasks come with none of that. 35-50s name it: this is task initiation, not laziness or inconsistency.
Payoff
"Her brain can do the hard thing. It just cannot generate the start on its own. So we built the start."
Show
b-roll: a departures board or a world map, then a full sink; a phone timer starting; the app's "just one thing" screen at the end.
On-screen
"capability is not the problem" / "initiation is" / "this is not laziness"
Call
Follow. No link on this one; let it land.
Why it works
The contradiction stops the scroll because it sounds impossible, then resolves into recognition. Caregivers have watched this exact gap and been told it was defiance. Naming it as initiation is a relief, and relief gets saved and shared.
Maddy
Named-only is plenty ("co-designed with Maddy"). Her words optional: one line she wrote about the gap.
Repurpose
The anchor for the YouTube origin film. Threads: the contradiction as a one-liner.

E / The "+" (the wider door, mechanism-level only)

Honesty boundary, hard line for this whole category

These three reach beyond AuDHD on purpose. The breadth is always carried by the mechanism as the subject ("the same wall," "executive function," "the research on task initiation"), never by the product. You may say "this kind of support is documented to help across these brains." You may never say "Maddy is proven to help long COVID." Name the mechanism, not Maddy.

Maddy's voice, at her rung

Used at the rung she chooses, Maddy's voice is the most credible content in the whole plan, because she is the exact person it was built with and for. This track is entirely opt-in (Section 01). Nothing here is a deliverable assigned to her. It is a menu of what becomes available when she wants it.

The co-design engine (recurring format, if she opts in)

A repeatable series where each post is one thing she asked for and why. It is endless, low-pressure, and it is the moat made visible:

  • "She asked for no red." Why failure colors hurt, what replaced them.
  • "She asked to see one thing, not twelve." The single-step view.
  • "She asked for a way to say I can't right now without it counting against her." The no-failure-state.
  • Each is a card in her voice (Dancing Script) plus your short context. Named or her-words rung. She approves each.

The translation rule

You translate her lived experience into content only with explicit permission, per piece. Her inner world is not a content farm. When in doubt, ask, and take the no easily.

The two of you, rarely

The two of you on screen is the most powerful and most depletable asset in the plan. The father-daughter dynamic is the thing no competitor can fake. It is also the thing that curdles fastest if overused or performed. So it runs on a scarcity rule.

The scarcity rule

A handful of together-pieces per year, never a weekly series. Each one is a genuine moment she opted into at "her presence" or "her lead," not a recurring obligation. Rarity is what keeps them real and keeps them landing.

Formats, when the moment is real

  • The feature test. 20 to 40 seconds of the two of you trying something in the app, honest reactions, including when it does not work.
  • The interview, her leading. She asks you why you built a thing, or you ask her what she would change. Unscripted, short.
  • The origin, told together. One piece, maybe once, where you both tell the story from your own sides. High-value, use sparingly.
  • The milestone. Launch day, a big review, a moment worth marking, together, if she wants to be there for it.

Every together-piece passes the full Section 01 flow: finished-asset review, cooling beat, two yeses, comment-shield pre-set, logged in JER-384.
